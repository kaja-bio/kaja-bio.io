Burger Day

Performed by Candlewasters, 2024

Burger Day touches on the topics of routine, mundanity, and the pointless repetition inherent to capitalistic exploitation of the working class in a strange post-apocalyptic setting. Characters trapped in a Burger King in a dystopian environment repeat the same tasks they did prior to the fall of society; a 'play within a play', they enact their roles much as they did before, until one of them finally snaps.
